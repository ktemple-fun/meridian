What We Fix

Garage Door Services in Meridian, MS

From broken springs to full door replacements, we handle every garage door issue for homeowners across Meridian and Lauderdale County.

🔩

Broken Spring Repair

A broken torsion or extension spring is the #1 reason garage doors won't open. We carry springs for all door sizes and can replace them the same day you call.

⚙️

Garage Door Opener Repair

Opener won't respond? Making grinding noises? We service and repair all major brands — Chamberlain, LiftMaster, Craftsman, Genie, and more.

🔗

Cable Repair & Replacement

Snapped or frayed cables make your door dangerous to operate. We'll replace your cables and check the full system before we leave.

🚨

Emergency Garage Door Repair

Door stuck open or won't close at all? We offer same-day emergency service across Meridian — because a door stuck open is a security issue.

🏠

New Garage Door Installation

Ready to upgrade? We install new residential garage doors in steel, carriage-style, and insulated options to fit your home and budget.

🔄

Tune-Up & Maintenance

A properly tuned door runs quieter, lasts longer, and costs less to operate. Our tune-up includes lubrication, balance check, safety sensor test, and hardware tightening.

📐

Panel Replacement

Dented or damaged panels don't always require a full door replacement. We can match and replace individual sections to restore your door's look and function.

🎛️

Opener Installation

Upgrading to a quieter belt drive or a smart opener you can control from your phone? We install and program all opener styles and brands.
